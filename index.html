What It Is All I Have • 2017
This song is basically about someone who's frustrated trying to get through to a stubborn person. It's like they've learned a lot over time, but the other person just laughs it off or doesn't get it. There's this feeling of wanting them to open up and understand—just for once. The lyrics are kind of poetic and emotional, talking about trying, feeling, and struggling. It's like a mix of hope, tension, and the wish that someday the other person will finally get it.

About

205 is an alternative rock band that emerged from the vibrant underground music scene. Founded in 2015, the band has been crafting a unique sound that blends raw energy with introspective lyrics. Their music explores themes of human connection, personal growth, and the complexities of modern life. With two albums under their belt - "All I Have" (2017) and "Ready or Not" (2024) - 205 continues to evolve their sound while staying true to their authentic approach to music-making. The band's name reflects their philosophy of embracing the present moment and creating music that speaks to the here and now.
